'output_summary_*.RData' contains ForCS summary outputs ('log_summary.csv') aggregated by management unit.
'ageMax_*.RData' contains the max age in each cell (from 'log_biomassC.csv').
'agbTotal_*.RData' contains ForCS aboveground biomass outputs for each cell ('log_biomassC.csv'), aggregated by species (all age-classes).
'output_BioToFPS_*.csv' contains total Biomass to Forest Product Sector (FPS) at every timestep
